Surface Profile

WVSPEC's optical profiling system measures surface roughness from 1 nm to tens of nm without contact. Our proprietary off-axis interferometry algorithm achieves sub-nanometer resolution, enabling real-time inline inspection of highly polished surfaces critical to semiconductor and electronics manufacturing.

●Sub-nm lateral resolution via off-axis interferometry
●Non-contact, high-speed — suitable for inline production
●Works on mirror-polished, chemically treated and structured surfaces

Transparent Film Thickness

Using spectral reflectance, our system measures transparent and matted thin film thickness inline — from single-layer dielectrics to complex multi-layer stacks. We consult to choose the right sensor and algorithm for your process.

●Spectral reflectance — broadband or single-wavelength modes
●Single & multi-layer transparent / matted films
●Inline & offline measurement; wafer-level mapping
●Process control integration (SPC / FDC compatible)

Analytic Material

Combining spectroscopy with deep learning, our inference platform provides qualitative and quantitative material analysis for absorption/matted materials. Custom models are built for defect detection, tomography, and outer/inner property characterisation.

●Qualitative & quantitative spectral analysis
●Deep learning inference — defect, tomography, properties
●Custom model development for specific applications
●Supports absorption and matted material types
